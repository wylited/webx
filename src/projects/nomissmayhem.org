#+title: No Miss Mayhem
#+filetags: :game:javascript:UIUX:

A fast paced, bullet hell, dungeon crawler game where /you are your own enemy/.

Created for [[https://counterspell.hackclub.com/][Counterspell HK]], in 8 hours, ranked 2nd regionally. [[https://airtable.com/appdpwk5T93TsDg4R/shrW4k6FcQPU5DtaK][Top 10]] globally, after continual development for a week.

The game engine is custom made in Javascript from scratch, alongside locally grown art and music.
